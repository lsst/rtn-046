\section{DM Science} \label{sec:dmsci}
The DM Science Team, led by the DM Lead Scientist, is responsible for validating the DMS scientifically, liaising with the community, optimizing the as-built system, and identifying new scientific opportunities.

This would also be the home for  cross-functional teams with no dedicated staff as  forums at which to raise issues and address cross-cutting topics, ensuring alignment with Rubin \& LSST science goals.
This group would provide science-driven prioritization of cross-department activities and global validation, trend analysis, KPI tracking, and anomaly detection  based on high-quality deliverables from each department.  Provides a forum at which to raise issues and address cross-cutting topics, provide cross-functional coordination, ensuring alignment with Rubin \& LSST science goals.

\subsection{DM Science Lead}
{\bf to end up in Staffing Sheet roles  and then appear in  \secref{sec:roles}}
The DM Scientist has the ultimate responsibility for ensuring DM initiatives provide solutions that meet the overall LSST science goals.
As such, this person leads the definition and understanding of the science goals and deliverables of the LSST Data Management and is accountable for communicating these to the DM engineering teams.

The DM Scientist  reports to the Rubin Director /
The DM Scientist  is a member of the DM and Rubin Change Control Boards and the Rubin Operations Science Team.
He/she chairs and directs the work of the DM  Science Team.

Specific responsibilities and authorities include:

\begin{itemize}
\item Communicates with DM science stakeholders (Rubin directors, Scientists and Team, advisory bodies, the science community) to understand their needs and identifies aspects to be satisfied by the DM Department.
\item Develops, maintains, and articulates the vision of DM products and services responsive to stakeholder needs.
\item Communicates the DM System vision to DM stakeholders. Works with the DMAD to communicate and articulate the DM System vision, requirements and especially priorities  to the DM  team.
\item Develops and/or evaluates proposed changes to DM deliverables driven by schedule, budget, or other constraints.
\item Validates the science quality of DM deliverables and the capability of all elements of the DM System to achieve LSST science goalsi with the V\&V team.

\item Serves as Data Management Liaison as requested by Science Collaborations or agrees delegates.

\item Defines the science acceptance criteria for DM deliverables (both final and intermediate) and validates that they have been met (Science Validation).
\item Delegates authority and responsibility as appropriate to institutional Science Leads and other DM science Team.
\item Represents and speaks for Rubin  Data Management.
\item Convenes and/or participates in all DM reviews.
\item Co-Chairs the DM Executive
\end{itemize}
